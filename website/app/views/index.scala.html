  • Give help, and get help, locally

    The Help Network revolves around your local community.

    You offer your time, and maybe more than that, to someone living in your city, or for a project happening nearby.

  • No money involved

    We don't believe in the current financial system, and want to disrupt it.

    Unless you provide something costly for someone or for a project, try your best to keep money out of it.

    Prefer alternatives such as gifting, alternative currency, or simply do it for free if you can!

  • It's about what you do

    The most important part of your user profile is the references of how you have been participating.

    Those references shouldn't be overlooked, as they are central to a healthy network where helpers are rewarded, and where abuse is kept at bay.

  • We are independant

    No VC funding. No advertising.

    And we respect your data: it won't be shared.
